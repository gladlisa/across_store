Our Story: Behind The Brand

Across Store, led by CEO Konstantin Kan, is a brand that seeks to inspire individuals of all ages and passions to break free from society's expectations and embrace their unique journeys.

Our collection features an array of stylish hoodies that cater to both the casual and sporty individual. We believe that outdoor sports and activities are meant to be enjoyed without unnecessary tension.

Our mission:

We strive to cultivate a sense of unity and encouragement among like-minded individuals who share a love for the great outdoors.
